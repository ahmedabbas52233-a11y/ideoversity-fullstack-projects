About Baroque

Redefining Pakistani fashion since 2010

Our Story

Baroque was founded with a vision to bring luxury Pakistani fashion to women who appreciate elegance, craftsmanship, and timeless design. What began as a small atelier in Lahore has grown into one of Pakistan's most beloved fashion houses.

Our collections blend traditional techniques with contemporary aesthetics, creating pieces that honor our heritage while embracing modern style. Every garment is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, from the selection of premium fabrics to the final stitch.

Today, Baroque stands as a symbol of sophistication in South Asian fashion, with a presence across Pakistan and a growing international clientele who seek authentic, high-quality Pakistani attire.

CRAFTSMANSHIP

QUALITY

We source only the finest fabrics and materials, ensuring each garment meets our exacting standards.

HERITAGE

Our designs celebrate Pakistani cultural heritage while embracing contemporary global fashion trends.
